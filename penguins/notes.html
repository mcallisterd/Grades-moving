The orange line through the middle of the graph is horizontal, at the grade that that particular penguin had as an average at the end of the course. The area above and below the line is color coded to show how the penguin was doing at the time relative to their final grade.
You can use the Y Zoom button to zoom in the visualization vertically to show a more close-up version of the same data, making small changes potentially more obvious.
To zoom in the x, give a starting x value and an ending x value, and we display the graph just over that range of x values, also zooming in the y. If you like weird geometric stuff, put the values in backwards, e.g. 40 in the first box and 0 in the second.
The pictures of penguins on the side are the way to navigate between different students, you can click on a penguin's picture to see their grades.
